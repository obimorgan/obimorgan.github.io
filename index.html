About Me


I am 33 years of age. Cheffing is my trade as mentioned in my CV. I have been cooking throughout most of my 20’s. I began learning to cook after I graduated from highschool when I got kicked out of my parent’s house and desperately needed to earn some income. I was lucky to be employed as a dishwasher at a friend’s family owned restaurant where I developed the love and passion for cooking. I enjoyed the 10 to 12 hour long shifts , the 3 to 4 hours of heavy stress during service, the sleep deprivations, the adrenaline kicks, and the sense of comradery we have among us “hospo” people.

Cheffing is very rewarding but tiring at times, I am ready to move on to a new line of work.

I stumbled upon programming during the first corona lock down in Oslo, Norway 2020. A friend of mine suggested that I should look into programming because I like solving problems. Little did I know learning how to code is a problem to be solved in itself. But I am enjoying it very much, I am excited and curious as to where this new found interest of mine can take me.
